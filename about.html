About me

Engineering Graduate

I’m an engineer, currently living in Toronto. A graduate of the University of Toronto, I’m interested in exploring the intersection of product, design, & data.

I Started in Academia!

My first internship was with the university’s Human Factors & Applied Statistics Lab where I investigated the social-psychological factors of distracted driving behavior. I also researched with the Microrobotics Lab, where I fabricated micro-scale robots for biomedical applications.

Transition to the Industry!

I’m currently working as a Product Designer at Tiercon, a Tier 1 manufacturer for the automotive industry. In the past, I’ve worked in procurement for a world-class automotive supplier. I’ve also worked as a Data Analyst for a global aerospace manufacturer.

Sports Enthusiast!

Outside of work, you can find me watching and playing football (or soccer, for my North American friends!). Besides soccer, I enjoy going on runs, and cheering on the Toronto Raptors!
