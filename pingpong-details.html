Low-Cost Ping Pong Launcher

Home Ping Pong Launcher

I worked collaboratively in a team to design a device that launches a ping pong ball into a plastic cup located at a variable distance between 1 to 4m. The device was designed as a low-cost toy for children. With the help of online simulation, the team integrated the following product design tools:

  • - Competitive Analysis
  • - Concept Generation and Concept Selection with a Pugh Matrix
  • - Product Functional Decomposition Diagram
  • - Economic Analysis
  • - P-Diagram
  • - Design FMEA
  • - Full Factorial Experiments
  • - Latitude Development
  • - 3D CAD Model and Engineering Drawings
  • - Tolerance Analysis
